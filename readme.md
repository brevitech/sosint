# N8RA
### Aggregated Strategic OSINT Platform

> N8RA is our aggregated strategic osint platform, purpose-built to deliver real-time situational awareness across active conflict theaters. Our inaugural deployment focuses on the US-Iran theater, architected with the same rigor and design philosophy behind our WorldMonitor project.

---

## 📡 Overview

N8RA is our answer to the fragmented OSINT landscape. We've engineered a premium multi-panel intelligence platform that consolidates real-time reporting from over 50 curated sources, renders interactive theater mapping, tracks military assets, monitors economic and sanctions activity, and surfaces dynamic threat assessments — all within a dark, military-grade UI built for operators.

We believe strategic intelligence shouldn't require enterprise contracts or API gatekeeping. **N8RA brings aggregated OSINT into a single pane of glass.**

---

## 🎯 Our Intent

N8RA is an intentful and honest attempt to build context for what ongoing conflict scenarios mean for **ASEAN nations and India**.

The global intelligence conversation is dominated by Western and Middle Eastern vantage points, leaving our region to interpret distant conflicts through borrowed lenses. Yet the second-order effects — energy security, maritime trade routes, diaspora exposure, diplomatic alignment, and economic shockwaves — land squarely on ASEAN and Indian shores.

We built N8RA to close that gap. Every module, every data source, and every design decision is oriented toward translating distant theater dynamics into intelligence that matters for decision-makers, analysts, and operators across ASEAN and India.

---

## 🏗️ Our Architecture

N8RA is built on a philosophy of **operational sovereignty** and **uncompromised performance**. We've deliberately engineered the platform to run lean, fast, and independent — free from the dependency bloat, vendor lock-in, and latency tax that plague modern intelligence tooling.

### Architectural Principles

| Principle | Description |
|---|---|
| **Sovereignty First** | No third-party frameworks, no opaque dependencies, no external runtime requirements. What we ship is what runs. |
| **Zero Gatekeeping** | No API keys, no paywalled feeds, no enterprise contracts standing between operators and the intelligence they need. |
| **Resilience by Design** | Critical asset and reference data is embedded directly in the build, ensuring core functionality persists even when upstream feeds degrade. |
| **Operator-Grade Performance** | Every layer is optimized for instant load, minimal footprint, and continuous uptime in low-bandwidth or contested environments. |
| **Modular Extensibility** | Each intelligence panel is an independent module, allowing us to extend, swap, or theater-shift without re-architecting the core. |

This foundation is what enables the **N8RA Modular Dashboard**.

## 🧩 The N8RA Modular Dashboard

We've structured the platform around twelve dedicated intelligence modules, each engineered as an independent panel within our unified operator view:

| # | Module | Function |
|---|---|---|
| 01 | **Live Intelligence Feed** | Real-time aggregated reporting from 50+ sources via our RSS proxy layer |
| 02 | **Theater Map** | Interactive map plotting military bases, naval assets, missile sites, and active conflict zones |
| 03 | **Military Assets Comparison** | Side-by-side US vs Iran force breakdowns across naval, air, ground, and nuclear domains |
| 04 | **Threat Level Matrix** | Dynamic escalation gauge paired with a historical timeline |
| 05 | **Strait of Hormuz Monitor** | Maritime chokepoint tracking and oil tanker flow analysis |
| 06 | **Sanctions & Economic Impact** | Sanctions tracker covering oil exports and Rial currency movements |
| 07 | **Nuclear Program Status** | Enrichment levels, IAEA inspection status, and facility mapping |
| 08 | **Regional Allies & Proxies** | Force disposition of Hezbollah, the Houthis, Iraqi militias, and Hamas |
| 09 | **Air Defense Networks** | Coverage overlays for S-300/S-400, Iron Dome, Patriot, and THAAD systems |
| 10 | **Naval Forces Tracker** | Carrier strike groups, Iranian naval assets, and Gulf deployments |
| 11 | **Cyber Operations** | US and Iranian cyber warfare history alongside the current threat landscape |
| 12 | **Timeline & Escalation History** | Interactive chronology of key events since 1979 |
